$Scope

$scope is where the magic in AngularJS happens.

$Scope can be initialized with default values

In a controller you can add

$scope.awesome_group = 'John Doe';

And in a view you can add

{{awesome_group}}

lets you put that awesome group in your template! Awesome Group: {{awesome_group}}

$Scope can watch changes
